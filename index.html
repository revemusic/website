NIIVA
Pop
Honest lyrics. Cinematic sound. Global reach.

NIIVA is a pop artist and songwriter known for her honest lyrics, cinematic sound, and powerful vocals. Born in Bulgaria and raised between London and Vancouver before settling in Toronto, her music blends global influences with personal storytelling.

She first gained international attention co-writing the record-breaking "Black Mamba" for K-pop group aespa, earning a SOCAN No. 1 Song Award. Her breakout single "Friends" charted on Canadian CHR/Top 40 radio at #13.

Recognized by iHeartRadio as a Future Star and KiSS RADiO as One to Watch, NIIVA continues to evolve, crafting music that resonates with listeners globally.

NYRE
Dark-Alt Pop
Cinematic hooks. Sharp storytelling. Emotional impact.

NYRE is a Toronto-based alt and dark-pop artist known for cinematic pop hooks, sharp storytelling, and emotionally charged songs that balance vulnerability with control. She broke through with "It Should Be You," winner of Toronto Honours' Single of the Year, and has earned multiple Spotify editorial placements on Fresh Finds Canada. Her catalogue has passed 1.25M streams and includes a Disney+/Hulu sync with "Youth" in How I Caught My Killer.

Her upcoming single "ME ON MY MIND" (March 20) is a confident pop record about choosing yourself after a relationship that made you feel like you had to get smaller to keep the peace. Written by NYRE with Sarah Boulton (AARYS) and producer Mike Schlosser, it captures the moment self-love stops being a concept and becomes a decision.

Our Story

About REVE MUSIC

REVE MUSIC is a Toronto-based artist management and development company focused on building artists with international potential. The company works closely with talent from the early stages of development through global releases, combining creative direction with long-term career strategy.

Founded by music industry veteran Tommy K.W. Lam, REVE MUSIC helps artists navigate today's evolving music business through strategic partnerships, digital growth, and international market expansion. The company's artists have achieved Billboard chart success, millions of global streams, major publishing deals, and international songwriting placements.

REVE MUSIC continues to focus on developing distinctive artists and connecting them with opportunities across North America, the UK, Europe, and Asia.

What Drives Us

REVE MUSIC is built on the idea that strong artist development leads to lasting success. By combining creative guidance with strategic industry relationships, we help artists move beyond local scenes and onto the global stage.

Tommy K.W. Lam
Founder and Artist Manager

Tommy K.W. Lam is a Toronto-based artist manager and founder of REVE MUSIC with nearly three decades of experience in the music industry. He began his career as a rock drummer before moving into production, digital media, and artist management. Over the years he has built a reputation for developing artists with global potential and guiding them from early stages to international success.

Tommy has played a key role in the rise of artists like NIIVA, whose work has earned multiple Billboard No. 1 chart achievements, millions of streams worldwide, and a SOCAN No. 1 Song Award for co-writing the K-pop hit Black Mamba by aespa. Earlier in his career, he co-founded Digital Groove and EXI Radio, pioneering internet radio in the 1990s, and later created FANPUSH, Canada's first crowdfunding platform for artists.

Today, he continues to focus on artist development, global strategy, and building long-term careers in music.
