Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 13, 2026

WAVR is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have over your information. We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Who We Are

WAVR is operated by Mind Vision LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company. References to "WAVR", "we", "us", or "our" in this Privacy Policy refer to Mind Vision LLC.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@wavr.app

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Artist profile data: Stage name, genre, influences, mood tags, release calendar, and other onboarding information you provide
  • Connected platform data: OAuth tokens, follower counts, engagement metrics, post performance data, and analytics from Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Spotify accounts you connect
  • Content data: Captions, hashtags, and other content you create, edit, or approve through WAVR
  • Usage data: How you interact with the WAVR platform, features used, and session data
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe. WAVR does not store your full payment card information
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the WAVR platform
  • Generate AI-powered content personalized to your artist identity
  • Schedule and publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Analyze content performance and provide analytics insights
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send service-related communications including receipts and updates
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your data to train general AI models or share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Connected Social Media Accounts

When you connect social media accounts to WAVR, we access data from those platforms solely to provide you with our services. Specifically:

  • Instagram / Facebook: We access your account via Meta's Graph API to publish content, retrieve analytics, and manage comments as you direct
  • TikTok: We access your account via TikTok's Content Posting API to publish content on your behalf
  • YouTube: We access your channel via Google's YouTube Data API for analytics and content publishing
  • Spotify for Artists: We access your artist data for audience insights and release calendar integration

OAuth tokens for connected accounts are stored securely and encrypted. You can revoke WAVR's access to any connected account at any time through your WAVR dashboard or directly through the relevant platform's settings.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We share data with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate WAVR, including Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), Clerk (authentication), Anthropic (AI content generation), and Vista Social (social media posting). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data
  • Platform APIs: Content and credentials are shared with connected social platforms (Meta, TikTok, Google) solely to fulfill publishing requests you initiate
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of WAVR and its users
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.

Analytics snapshots and aggregated performance data may be retained in anonymized form after account deletion.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption at rest and in transit, secure OAuth token storage, and access controls limiting who can access your data within our organization. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent: Disconnect connected accounts or delete your WAVR account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@wavr.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Tracking

WAVR uses essential cookies necessary for the platform to function, including session authentication cookies. We also use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our platform. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, though disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

WAVR is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

11. International Data Transfers

WAVR operates primarily in the United States and Canada. If you access our Service from outside these regions, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using WAVR, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, via email. Your continued use of WAVR after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
