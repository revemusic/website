NIIVA
Pop
Honest lyrics. Cinematic sound. Global reach.

NIIVA is a pop artist and songwriter known for her honest lyrics, cinematic sound, and powerful vocals. Born in Bulgaria and raised between London and Vancouver before settling in Toronto, her music blends global influences with personal storytelling.

She first gained international attention co-writing the record-breaking "Black Mamba" for K-pop group aespa, earning a SOCAN No. 1 Song Award. As a solo artist, her releases have racked up millions of streams and appeared on major playlists worldwide. Her breakout single "Friends" charted on Canadian CHR/Top 40 radio at #13 and helped establish her as a rising pop voice.

Recognized by iHeartRadio as a Future Star and KiSS RADiO as One to Watch, NIIVA continues to evolve, crafting music that resonates with listeners globally.

NYRE
Dark-Alt Pop
Cinematic hooks. Sharp storytelling. Emotional impact.

NYRE is a Toronto-based alt and dark-pop artist known for cinematic pop hooks, sharp storytelling, and emotionally charged songs that balance vulnerability with control. She broke through with "It Should Be You," winner of Toronto Honours' Single of the Year, and has earned multiple Spotify editorial placements on Fresh Finds Canada. Her catalogue has passed 1.25M streams and includes a Disney+/Hulu sync with "Youth" in How I Caught My Killer.

Her upcoming single "ME ON MY MIND" (March 20) is a confident pop record about choosing yourself after a relationship that made you feel like you had to get smaller to keep the peace. Written by NYRE with Sarah Boulton (AARYS) and producer Mike Schlosser, it captures the moment self-love stops being a concept and becomes a decision. With a bold visual world led by Vincent Miller, NYRE is entering a new era built for impact.

Our Story
"Building artists with international potential — one relationship at a time."

RÊVE MUSIC is a Toronto-based artist management and development company focused on building artists with international potential. The company works closely with talent from the early stages of development through global releases, combining creative direction with long-term career strategy.

Founded by music industry veteran Tommy K.W. Lam, RÊVE MUSIC helps artists navigate today's evolving music business through strategic partnerships, digital growth, and international market expansion. The company's artists have achieved Billboard chart success, millions of global streams, major publishing deals, and international songwriting placements.

RÊVE MUSIC continues to focus on developing distinctive artists and connecting them with opportunities across North America, the UK, Europe, and Asia.

What drives us

We believe the best artist-manager relationships are built on trust, transparency, and shared vision. Not every artist needs the same path — and our job is to understand yours deeply enough to help you build it.

From Toronto's independent music scene to the boardrooms of Seoul, we've spent decades building the kind of relationships that open doors no cold call ever could.

Tommy K.W. Lam
Founder & Artist Manager

Tommy K.W. Lam is a Toronto-based artist manager and founder of RÊVE MUSIC with nearly three decades of experience in the music industry.

He began his career as a rock drummer before moving into production, digital media, and artist management. Over the years he has built a reputation for developing artists with global potential and guiding them from early stages to international success.

Tommy has played a key role in the rise of artists like NIIVA, whose work has earned multiple Billboard No. 1 chart achievements, millions of streams worldwide, and a SOCAN No. 1 Song Award for co-writing the K-pop hit Black Mamba by aespa. Today, he continues to focus on artist development, global strategy, and building long-term careers in music.
